A bit about me, and the things I do (or have done)

Story so far

I began my career as a software engineer at Cognizant, specializing in full-stack tech, while working on projects of various scales in the financial domain. During this time, I moved to Belgium and started working on-site for KBC bank as a front-end developer.

After two years of work experience, I decided to pivot my career and started pursuing a Masters in Artificial Intelligence at KU Leuven, specializing in Big Data Programming. My master’s thesis was focused on developing a billings and settlements system for car-sharing in a completely decentralized fashion. This is what got me into the field of blockchains.

In the summer of 2018, I successfully defended my master’s thesis, and decided to pursue a Ph.D., supervised by Prof. Bart Preneel, focusing on a blend between Cryptography and Blockchains at COSIC, KU Leuven. I chose COSIC because of its reputation as a world-class group in the field of cryptography and the good experience I had while being supervised by COSIC researchers.

In the winter of 2022, I decided to combine by Ph.D. with a full-time job (yes, I am that crazy), and accepted Tomer Ashur's offer to join him at 3MI Labs.

In the spring of 2024 I successfuly defended my PhD thesis, and subsequently got promoted to the CTO position at 3MI Labs.
